# Kubernetes Engineer

## About the Role

We are looking for a Kubernetes Engineer to join our Platform Engineering team. You will be responsible for designing, deploying, and maintaining our container orchestration infrastructure. This role is critical to ensuring our applications run reliably and efficiently at scale across multiple environments.

## Responsibilities

- Design, deploy, and manage production-grade Kubernetes clusters
- Implement and maintain CI/CD pipelines for containerized applications
- Develop and enforce best practices for container security and compliance
- Monitor cluster health, performance, and capacity planning
- Create and maintain Infrastructure as Code (IaC) using Terraform, Helm, and Kustomize
- Troubleshoot complex issues across networking, storage, and compute layers
- Collaborate with development teams to optimize application deployments
- Implement service mesh solutions and manage ingress/egress traffic
- Automate operational tasks and improve platform reliability
- Document architecture, runbooks, and operational procedures

## Requirements

### Must Have

- 3+ years of experience with Kubernetes in production environments
- Strong understanding of container technologies (Docker, containerd)
- Experience with cloud providers (AWS EKS, Azure AKS, or GCP GKE)
- Proficiency in Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Pulumi, or CloudFormation)
- Experience with Helm charts and Kubernetes manifest management
- Solid understanding of networking concepts (DNS, load balancing, CNI plugins)
- Experience with monitoring and observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK)
- Strong scripting skills (Bash, Python, or Go)
- Understanding of security best practices and RBAC

### Nice to Have

- Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) or Certified Kubernetes Application Developer (CKAD)
- Experience with GitOps tools (ArgoCD, Flux)
- Knowledge of service mesh technologies (Istio, Linkerd)
- Experience with multi-cluster management and federation
- Familiarity with operators and custom resource definitions (CRDs)
- Experience with secrets management (Vault, External Secrets)
- Knowledge of cost optimization strategies for Kubernetes
- Experience with chaos engineering practices

## Tech Stack

- Kubernetes (EKS/AKS/GKE)

- Terraform, Helm, Kustomize
- ArgoCD, GitHub Actions
